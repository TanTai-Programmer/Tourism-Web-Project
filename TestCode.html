Rowing

Boating on Ha Long sea is an extremely interesting sea experience. Visitors can enjoy controlling their small boat to the wide seas.
Or if you are a lover of exploring caves, try going to Luon cave, Co cave, Ba Trai Dao, Dau Be, Ba Ham which are suitable places for visitors to kayak.
